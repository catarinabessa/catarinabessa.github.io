description

botw tarot card is an illustration that reimagines link from the game "breath of the wild" as a genderbent character.
in the artwork, link is depicted wearing the barbarian armor, a striking outfit from the game known for its fierce and rugged design.
the illustration is framed within the context of the strength card from the tarot deck, symbolizing courage and resilience

created in 2021, using paint tool sai 2 on a wacom intuos comic m pen tablet.
